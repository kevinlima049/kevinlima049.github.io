ABOUT ME

I first got interested into computers when I was about 9 years old, my dad was a computer programmer and my brother was obsessed with video games, so eventually technology was just a usual part of my life. My dad always wanted me to do something with graphic design and that’s what I became really interested and that introduced me to the technology production I’m currently enrolled in. The technology production program I’m enrolled in offers me a large variety of programs to work and get better with, which is really beneficial to me and I’ve been really enjoying it. I’ve became certified in Adobe Illustrator CC and hopefully there is more to come to the certification list. What I became mostly interested in is coding. I’m love the idea of designing a program or a website completely from scratch, just the ideal of being able to modify and create something out of nothing fascinates me, and I’m really excited to be able to learn that soon and get better at it so eventually some internship and job positions open up for me with all the skills that I’ve acquired from the program.
